Courier Services

We offer Air Freight Import and Export Services to all regions of the world including: services to Latin America (Central and South America) with offices in Santiago, Chile, Ciudad del Este and Asuncion, Paraguay. The Caribbean, Europe (Western, Central and Eastern), Asia (including Central Asia / Taiwan), countries aroun Africa and many more destinations. Let us be part of your team for all air transport of your goods. If you need it fast we are aligned with the majority of the well known airlines in the industry. We can handle any size shipment anywhere in the world.

 

No shipment is too specialized.

Not only can we meet your budget and schedule requests, you can track your shipments every step of the way to ensure our customers receive their orders as promised. We can also help design an airfreight solution that meets your needs from the United States and around the world. Call our dedicated team today and see for yourself. We have a reliable network of worldwide agents that cover a vast area of the world and allow us door to door capabilities including destination customs clearance/duties/taxes.
