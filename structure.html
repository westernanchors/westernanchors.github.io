Western Anchors Company’s structure

+ Fleet Operation Department

  • Cargo and passenger marine transportations;
  • Efficient operation with the fleet;
  • Brokerage and chartering services;
  • Customs warehouse services and customs carrier services.

+ Navigation Safety Department

  • The Company’s safety and quality management system supporting;
  • Monitoring of compliance with the applicable international and national
  • regulations onboard the company’s vessels and in the office;
  • Accidents and injuries prevention programs. Registration, investigation and analysis
  • of accidents and injuries on board the company’s vessels;
  • Providing with charts, navigational and meteorological support for safe shipping;
  • The company’s  IT system support, development of own software products for the vessels and the office;
  • Navigation, communication and GMDSS equipment installation and maintenance;
  • Fire safety monitoring.

+ Technical Department

  • Technical  management of bulk carries, passenger ships, general cargo carriers;
  • Vessel technical condition monitoring;
  • Navigation and telecommunication equipment maintenance.

+ Financial Department

  • Economic support of the company’s activities;
  • Cooperation with financial and banking institutions;
  • Budgeting;
  • Foreign currency transactions;
  • Efficient system for corporate property management;
  • Business-monitoring of the competitors in the spheres of company’s interests.
  • Preparing of business plans, new projects and activities.

+ Account Department

  • Compiling accounts on the MSCo activities and property status;
  • Financial and economic activities analysis;
  • Control over the safety of the shareholders' property;
  • The Company Tax Policy management.

+ Human Resources Department

  • HR policy and recruitment ;
  • Realization of social policy of the company;
  • Personnel training (initial training, additional training, advanced training).

+ Legal Division

  • legal protection of the Company’s interests in disputes with the third parties and in court;
  • Legal consulting and supporting for  activities MSCo’s departments;
  • Insurance protections in relations with the third parties and in court.

+ Control and Administration Division

  • Economic security.

+ Press Centre

  • Advertising and promoting of the company.
  • MSCo museum.

- Corporate Relations division

  • Dealing with shareholders, analysis and assessment of the stock capital.
