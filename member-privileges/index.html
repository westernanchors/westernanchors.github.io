Member Privileges

SECURITY

Our state-of-the-art facility provides unparalleled security for precious commodities. From 24-hour physical monitoring to biometric identification and sophisticated motion detection systems, Western Anchors ensures unparalleled security at all times.

INSURANCE

Western Anchors goes beyond the physical safeguarding of your precious valuables by providing full liability insurance for your complete peace of mind. 

 

Our insurance brokers insure Western Anchors for safe deposit box contents under the comprehensive Western Anchors Liability Policy. Under this policy, all Western Anchors safety deposit boxes are insured for a minimum of £10,000 at no cost to our client. Certificates of Insurable Interest are available on request. 

PRIVATE VIEWING SUITE

Our private viewing suite has been designed to create a discreet environment to inspect and access your personal safety deposit box. This suite can be booked online at any time for your convenience. Clients with a reservation will be given priority access, however clients may visit the vault at anytime during working hours, 6 days a week without prior notice.

INVENTORY REVIEW

Western Anchors provides independent review of bullion stored in your private safety deposit box. Through our partners, our experts have a detailed knowledge of the precious metals market.   Our Inventory Review process has been specifically developed to achieve consistency, objectivity and accountability in strict compliance with professional standards and is utilised by accountants and self-managed super funds (SMSF) as part of annual reporting requirements. 

 

Our Inventory Review process involves our Group Compliance Officer executing an itemised statutory declaration of your bullion inventory.  This  meets the necessary requirements of your accountant or financial advisor. Additional fees apply for this service. 

LOGISTICS

Western Anchors provides secure, convenient door-to-door transportation of your precious commodities to or from the vault. Whether international, interstate or local, our logistics network provides transportation of your contents anywhere in the world including supervision by an independent representative. Additional fees apply for this service.
