Whether bullion, jewellery, or confidential documents - your valuables will receive first-class protection at Western Anchors. Safety deposit boxes can only be accessed with your unique security key and  bio-metric identification.  Your authorised individuals must adhere to the same strict entry requirements.  Western Anchors has no access to your vault which provides you complete privacy and security assurance.
