How to Send a Package and Get Started with US

Details are critical. Ease is a priority. And dependability is key.

From the smallest box to the largest pallet, we’re here to help you get those important shipments where they need to go, when you need them there.

And no matter how frequently you ship, whether once a year or every day, we’ve got services designed for you.

I SHIP INFREQUENTLYI SHIP WEEKLYI SHIP EVERY DAY
I SHIP INFREQUENTLY

If you only ship a few times a month or less, we welcome you to ship and track packages as a guest. Schedule a collection whenever you need one, or drop off packages at a nearby location.

Of course, you can always sign up for a Arabann Shipping UAE.com ID and save frequently used info like contacts and payment methods to your profile. Use your ID to:

  • Access your shipping and collection history
  • Set preferences and make repeated tasks easier
  • Save up to 50 tracked shipments
  • Order the supplies needed to package your items
I SHIP WEEKLY

If you ship a few times a week, consider these suggestions in addition to intercontinentaldeliveryservices.com shipping and tracking:

Set up weekly billing and charge Arabann Shipping UAE services to a payment account.

Add automatic tracking notifications for up to five people.

Help to make return shipments simple for your customers.

Estimate landed cost, find harmonised codes and manage your international shipping compliance.

I SHIP EVERY DAY

If shipping is a key business task and you send 25 or more packages a week, we recommend that you:

Set up weekly billing and charge Arabann Shipping UAE services to a payment account.

Download our shipping software and automate processes, import customer details and export shipping data.

Watch all inbound and outbound shipments on a comprehensive tracking dashboard.

Talk to one of our representatives and learn more about how we can help to solve your specific needs.
