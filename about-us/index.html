About Western Anchors

Western Anchors confidential and detailed understanding of clients’ needs has been developed on decades of experience and knowledge gained through Pallion, United Kingdom's largest independent precious metal services group.

OUR MISSION

Western Anchors' mission centres on providing secure safety deposit box solutions to deliver our clients unrivalled service, security and privacy. Independent of the United Kingdom banking system, Western Anchors offers the region’s most convenient way to store everything from bullion to documents, jewellery, hard drives and other valuables. Our logistics network can provide transportation of your contents anywhere in the world.

 

The Western Anchors facility has been designed and constructed from the ground up and incorporates a unique combination of physical surveillance, cutting-edge technology and superior security including bio-metric identification, pin code access, 24-hour CCTV monitoring, alarm and fire control systems.

 

SECURITY

At Western Anchors, security is paramount. Our state of the art facility provides unparalleled security for your precious commodities. From 24-hour physical monitoring, to bio-metric identification and sophisticated motion detection systems, Western Anchors ensures unparalleled security at all times whilst maintaining strict adherence to United Kingdom federal security regulations.

DISCRETION

Western Anchors offers complete discretion at every level.

-  Discrete Londonlocation

-  Secure private access

-  Private viewing suite

-  Personnel who appreciate the importance of privacy and confidentiality

INDEPENDENCE

Western Anchors is an independent United Kingdom private vaulting facility.

We operate outside of the United Kingdom banking system, ensuring you are able to control and manage your assets with complete freedom and subject to our comprehensive privacy policy

 

Self Storage Association of United Kingdom Membership

 

Western Anchors is proud to announce our membership to the SSAA - the pre-eminent association for the storage industry across United Kingdom and Asia.  Under this membership Western Anchors will continue to maintain best practice standards, avoid practices that do not support environmental sustainability, ensure high levels of safety for our employees, clients and visitors and to ensure our staff are competent in their duties and complete appropriate training in best practice standards.
